Jun Yoo

Doctoral Candidate in Finance

The Graduate Center, CUNY & Baruch College, Zicklin School of Business

I study how information frictions and regulatory design shape small business credit markets. My work examines lender specialization, discretion, and the role of soft information.

I am a recipient of the Graduate Center Dissertation Year Fellowship and the Connect New York Summer Research Fellowship.
